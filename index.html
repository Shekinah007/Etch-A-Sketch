Etch-A-Sketch

    Instructions

  • Click and drag cursor to interact with canvas.
  • Alternatively, hold "Shift" on your keyboard and drag cursor to do same.
  • You can change the color of the canvas and brush using the color pickers.
  • Toggle the eraser tool by pressing "E" on your keyboard or clicking on the icon.
  • Toggle grid line visibility using the button.
  • Use the slider to adjust grid size.
  • Use the button to clear the canvas.

      • Note: Changing the grid size will also clear the canvas.

  • Click to download image.
  • Press "M" on your keyboard to toggle between day/night themes.
